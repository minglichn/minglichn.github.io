    Publications

    Since 2011, I have published 38 articles with collaborators in peer-reviewed journals and a book. These include: Physical Review E (3), National Science Review (1), Physics Reports (1), New Journal of Physics (2), Journal of Statistical Mechanics (4), Chaos, Solitons & Fractals (1), Europhysics Letters (3), Physics Letters A (1), PLoS ONE (1), Scientific Reports (3), Physica A (8), Chinese Physics Letters (1), Chinese Physics B (4), conference papers (2), and journals in Chinese (3).
